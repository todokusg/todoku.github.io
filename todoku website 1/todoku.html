Our Story

In Japanese, Todoku (届く) means "to arrive" and that is the promise we make to you.

We are a proxy shopping service created for Singaporeans who want access to products that are only available in Japan. Founded by two Singaporean university students studying in Japan, we wanted to make Japanese products more accessible without the need to break the bank to specifically come here to get them.

From limited-edition merchandise and collectibles to beauty products, fashion, and everyday essentials, we help you purchase directly from Japan with ease and confidence. We handle the entire process, from sourcing and purchasing to shipping, so you can enjoy authentic Japanese products hassle-free.

We know what it is like to spot something in Japan and have no way to get it. That is why we started Todoku: tell us what you want, and we will take care of the rest!

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We have got answers!

A proxy service acts as your representative in Japan. We purchase items on your behalf from stores that do not ship internationally, then forward them to you in Singapore. You tell us what you want and we handle everything else!
We can purchase from most platforms including Mercari Japan, Yahoo Auctions Japan, Rakuten, Amazon Japan, brand official websites, and physical stores. If you are unsure whether we can get something specific, just ask us before ordering!
Shipping from Japan to Singapore typically takes one to two weeks depending on the method selected. We offer options including EMS and air parcel, and will recommend the best fit for your item and timeline.
We accept PayNow and Singapore Bank Transfers. We will send you an invoice for the first payment (item cost plus shipping to us) first, followed by a second payment invoice after we confirm receipt of your item.
If an item becomes unavailable after payment, you will receive a full refund. No worries!
We are unable to purchase items that are prohibited for export or import. This includes, but is not limited to, weapons, certain plants, pornography, tobacco, and other restricted goods. If you are unsure, please check with us before placing an order.
Once your item ships from Japan, we will send you a tracking number so you can follow its progress all the way to your door. We also send you updates at key milestones along the way!
